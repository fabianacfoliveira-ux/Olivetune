About Fabiana

Hello! I'm Fabiana, founder of Olivetune. My journey has been one of transformation and continuous learning.

For 14 years, I worked in hospitality and restaurant management, dedicating myself to creating meaningful experiences for others. However, in 2020, I made a pivotal decision to follow my intuition and begin my practice as a holistic therapist. This transition marked the beginning of a profound journey of self-discovery and spiritual growth.

Since then, my practice has been a constant journey of learning, deepening, and evolving. I am an intuitive person who works with the energy of unconditional love. Every session, every workshop, and every interaction is guided by this principle—to help you reconnect with your inner wisdom, heal your traumas, and unlock your true potential.

At Olivetune, we believe that true wellness comes from honoring the connection between your body, mind, and spirit. I'm here to guide you on this transformative path with compassion, intuition, and the healing power of unconditional love.
